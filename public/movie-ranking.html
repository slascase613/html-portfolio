The Best Movies According to Samuel

My top 3 movies off all-time

A Prince in New York

This is my favorite movie.I like that movie because of the love story told in it. A prince that put himself in the most humble appearance to find a girl that would love him for himself and not for his royalty.

Transformers

Intelligent machine transformable into robot and car at the same time is fascinating. That's what makes me like that movie.

Maman j’ai raté l’avion 2

This is the story of a little boy who got on the wrong plane while traveling with his extended family. He's spotted by 2 thieves, who have a hard time with him as he sets traps for them. "Home Alone" is the original title of this movie.
